About the Fellowship

Science Corps fellows are PhD scientists who spend a minimum of six months living and working at one of our partner institutions in an underserved community. Fellows teach hands-on science, develop curriculum, train local educators, and contribute to building lasting scientific capacity at their host site.

This is not a research fellowship — it is a teaching and science education fellowship. We are looking for scientists who are passionate about sharing their knowledge and skills with students and communities that have limited access to high-quality STEM education.

Eligibility

  • Holds a PhD in a scientific field (any discipline)
  • Completed their doctorate within the last four years
  • Available for a minimum of six months at a host site
  • Passionate about science education and community engagement
  • Adaptable, collaborative, and willing to live and work in a new cultural context

Current Host Sites

Applications are currently open for placements at our two partner host sites:

  • CVIF-DLS — Center for Vivid Imaginations Foundation – De La Salle, Philippines
  • Aavishkaar — Aavishkaar Science Education Centre, Palampur, Himachal Pradesh, India

What Fellows Receive

  • Monthly living stipend calibrated to local cost of living
  • Housing assistance at or near the host site
  • Community of Science Corps fellows and alumni
  • Mentorship and professional development support
  • A transformative experience teaching science in a new cultural context

The Next Cohort

Applications for our June 2026 cohort are now open. We review applications on a rolling basis, so we encourage you to apply early. The selection process includes a written application, an interview, and reference checks.

How to Apply

To apply, please send the following to fellows@science-corps.org:

  1. A cover letter (up to 2 pages) describing your background, why you want to be a Science Corps fellow, and which host site(s) you are interested in
  2. Your CV or resume
  3. Contact information for two references (we will contact them if you advance to the interview stage)
Apply by Email

Questions?

If you have questions about the fellowship or the application process, please contact us. You can also read about our current and former fellows to get a better sense of the fellowship experience.
