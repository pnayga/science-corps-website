Photos from Gaby's Fellowship at Aavishkaar, India

February 28, 2020  ·  Fellow Update  ·  Science Corps

Gabriela (Gaby) completed her Science Corps fellowship at Aavishkaar in Palampur, Himachal Pradesh, India — a science education center nestled in the Himalayan foothills. Below is a collection of photos documenting her fellowship experience: student workshops, field activities, and the vibrant scientific community at Aavishkaar.

Aavishkaar has been a Science Corps partner since our early days, providing a home for fellows who want to bring hands-on science education to students in one of India's most beautiful and culturally rich regions.
