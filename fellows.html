Adriane McDonald

CVIF-DLS  ·  Philippines  ·  2025–present

She is currently serving as a Science Corps fellow at the Center for Vivid Imaginations Foundation – De La Salle (CVIF-DLS) in the Philippines, where she teaches hands-on science and develops curriculum for students who otherwise have limited access to quality STEM education.
