What We Do

About Us

Who We Are

Science Corps is a nonprofit that pairs PhD scientists with host institutions in underserved regions around the world. Our fellows teach, mentor, and build scientific infrastructure where it is needed most.

Learn more →
Fellowship

Becoming a Fellow

Recent PhD graduates in STEM are placed at partner institutions for 3–12 months. Fellows design curricula, teach courses, and run hands-on experiments that give students real scientific experience.
