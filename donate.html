Thank You for Supporting Science Corps!

Your contributions help us send recent PhD graduates in STEM to teach and develop scientific capacity in underserved areas as Science Corps Fellows. Fellows leverage their expertise to support local schools and projects by designing scientific curricula, teaching, hosting workshops, and demonstrating experiments that provide students with hands-on STEM experience.
