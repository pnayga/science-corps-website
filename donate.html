Help us bring world-class scientists to underserved communities

Why Your Gift Matters

Science Corps is a small nonprofit with an outsized mission: connecting the world's most highly trained scientists with the students and communities that can benefit most from their expertise. Your donation makes that possible.

Gifts to Science Corps directly fund fellow stipends, curriculum development, and the operational costs of running the program. Every dollar goes toward building scientific capacity in communities that need it most.

What Your Gift Supports

$50

Covers the cost of hands-on science materials for one student workshop at a partner site.

$250

Supports one week of a Science Corps fellow's living stipend as they teach and mentor students full-time.

$1,000

Funds an entire month of hands-on science programming at a partner host site, reaching dozens of students.

Make a Donation

Science Corps is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the extent permitted by law.

To make a donation, please contact us at fellows@science-corps.org or use the button below. We are working on setting up an online donation portal — in the meantime, we accept donations by check (made payable to "Science Corps") or bank transfer.

Other Ways to Support Science Corps

  • Spread the word — Share our mission with scientists and institutions that might want to get involved.
  • Host a fellow — If your organization works in science education in an underserved community, contact us about becoming a host site.
  • Become a fellow — If you are a PhD scientist within four years of your doctorate, apply to be a fellow.

Science Corps places PhD scientists in underserved communities worldwide to teach STEM and build scientific capacity.
